About Us

We built the tool we kept wishing existed

Professional SEO audits should not cost $200 a month before you have ten clients. We disagreed with that — so we fixed it.

Our story

SEO Inspector Hub started from a straightforward frustration. Agencies and freelancers doing SEO work need two things consistently: a reliable audit that covers what actually matters, and a way to present that audit to clients without the tool's branding making it obvious where the data came from.

Most platforms that solve the second problem charge between $79 and $500 per month — and bundle the branding removal feature into dashboards, rank trackers, and client portals that newer agencies either do not need yet or cannot afford to pay for before their revenue justifies it.

We built SEO Inspector Hub to remove that constraint. The audit runs 60+ checks across eight SEO categories. The white label reports come out as clean PDF and Word files with no platform branding, no watermark, and no footer attribution. There is no account to create, no subscription to start, and no limit on how many reports you run.

The tools cover what has changed in search — not just on-page and technical SEO, but also AEO readiness (how well a page is structured to appear in AI-generated answers) and GEO readiness (how well a site signals to large language models that its content is citable and authoritative). These are not future concerns. They are ranking and visibility factors right now.

What we do not compromise on
01
The reports are genuinely unbranded
Not "mostly unbranded with a small footer." Zero attribution to SEO Inspector Hub in any download. The PDF and Word files you give to clients carry only what you put in them.
02
The audit covers what search engines actually use
Core Web Vitals from Google Lighthouse, real robots.txt parsing, security header detection, structured data validation, AEO scoring, and GEO readiness signals. Not a checklist from 2018.
03
No account, no email, no friction
Enter a URL, run the audit, download the report. That is the entire process. We do not collect emails before showing results or ask you to verify anything before downloading.
04
Free means free — not free with a catch
No per-report fee after a trial period. No "first 3 reports free." No feature lock that makes the free version useless. The core tool is free because the people who need it most are often the ones with the smallest budget for software.
